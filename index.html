About the Seahawks

//This and the things under are the pargraphs

The Seattle Seahawks are a professional American football team located in Seattle. They are a part of the NFC West division. The team joined the NFL in 1976 as an expansion team.

There fanbase is known as the "12s" the Seahawks have a rich history, including winning Super Bowl XLVIII. Their team colors are Navy, Lime Green, and Grey, representing the Pacific Northwest.

From thrilling victories to memorable players, the Seahawks have created countless moments for their FANS GO 12. This website is dedicated to celebrating that spirit and staying connected connected with everything Seahawks.
