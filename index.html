About the Seahawks

The Seattle Seahawks are a professional American football team based in Seattle. They compete in the National Football League (NFL) as part of the NFC West division. The team joined the NFL in 1976 as an expansion team.

Known for their passionate fanbase, the "12s," the Seahawks have a rich history, including winning Super Bowl XLVIII. Their team colors are College Navy, Action Green, and Wolf Grey, representing the Pacific Northwest.

From thrilling victories to memorable players, the Seahawks have created countless moments for their loyal supporters. This website is dedicated to celebrating that spirit and keeping you connected with everything Seahawks.
